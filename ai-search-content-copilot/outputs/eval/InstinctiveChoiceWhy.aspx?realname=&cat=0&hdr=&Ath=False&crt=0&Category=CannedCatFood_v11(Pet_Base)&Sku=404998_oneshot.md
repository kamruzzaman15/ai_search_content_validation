# Product Overview

Since 2006, Instinctive Choice has been a feline favorite, making every mealtime an occasion. This premium canned cat food is renowned for its balanced nutrition and delectable flavors, ensuring your cat gets a diet that's as yummy as it is healthy.

## Formulation Philosophy

At Instinctive Choice, our commitment to crafting the perfect meal begins with high-quality proteins. We believe in providing cats with a blend of succulent chicken, turkey, and trout, simmered in rich chicken broth for an irresistible aroma and gourmet flavor that cats instinctively crave. Our philosophy is centered around ensuring your cat's meals are not only delicious but also nutritionally balanced to support their overall health.

## Key Ingredients

- **High-Quality Proteins:** Chicken, Turkey, Trout
- **Chicken Broth:** Rich in flavor and nutrients.
- **Salmon Oil (Preserved With Mixed Tocopherols):** Provides essential omega-6 and omega-3 fatty acids for skin and coat health.
- **Natural Flavor:** Adds a natural taste that cats love.

## Differentiators

- **Moisture Content:** Crafted with added moisture to ensure your cat stays well-hydrated, happy, and in tip-top shape through all life stages.
- **Texture:** Soft pate-style texture guarantees content purrs and clean bowls.
- **Guaranteed Analysis:** Meets AAFCO Cat Food Nutrient Profiles for all life stages.

## Use Cases

- **Picky Eaters:** Perfect for cats who are selective about their food.
- **Urinary Health:** Hydration-rich formula supports urinary health.
- **All Life Stages:** Suitable for adult cats, kittens, and pregnant/lactating females.

## Competitive Positioning

- **Grain Free:** No corn, wheat, or soy.
- **Carrageenan Free:** BPA-free recyclable can.
- **No Harmful Additives:** Carrageenan free, grain free, no harmful preservatives.

## Claims Requiring Review

- **Nutritional Levels Established by AAFCO:** Verify that the product meets these established levels for all life stages.
